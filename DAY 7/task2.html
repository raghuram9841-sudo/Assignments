Step To Cook Meenkulambu

  1. Clean and wash your fish Rub the fish with turmeric and salt and set it aside to marinate for at least 15-30 minutes, soak a lime-sized ball of tamarind in about one cup of warm water for 15-30 minutes, extract the tararind pulp by squeezing the mixture with your hands. strain it and set the juice aside

  2. Heat gingely oil in a heavy-bottomed pan or clay pot. add mustard seeds and fenugreek seeds and let them splutter add shallots and garlic cloves saute until the shallots turn sift and light brown add curry leaves and tamatoes are soft and mushy Stir in your spice powder such as red chilli powder coriander powder and turmeric cook on low heat for a minute to prevent burnning

  3. Pour the extracted tamarind juicie into the sauteed masala base Bring the gravy to a boil the reduce disappers the heat and let it simmer to about 10-15 minute until the raw smell of the spices and oil begins separate and float on top
